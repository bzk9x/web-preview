Our Philosophy

Crafted for
the long life

Amethyst was born from a refusal — a refusal to accept that beautiful things must be temporary. Each piece begins with weeks of material study, months of prototyping, and an uncompromising conversation between design and function.

Our ateliers in Milan and Lagos work in continuous dialogue — bringing European tradition and West African spatial sensibility into a singular language of restraint and warmth.

How We Make

Craft at
every step

01

Material Selection

Every journey begins with raw materials — saddle leathers sourced from Italian tanneries, hardwoods certified by FSC, and performance fabrics woven to last decades not seasons.

02

Structural Engineering

Our frames are jointed, not stapled. Mortise-and-tenon construction from the first prototype ensures structural integrity across years of generous living.

03

Master Upholstery

Hand-tied spring systems and hand-layered foams by artisans who have trained for years in the specific language of luxury seating.

04

Quality Assurance

Every piece passes a 47-point inspection before leaving the atelier. Our warranty doesn't begin at purchase — it begins at the first stitch.
